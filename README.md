## 📄 Overview

<a href="#otterAi">Otter AI</a> serves professional teams, remote and hybrid workers, journalists, researchers, students, and anyone whose work involves conversations and meetings that need to be documented — the growing population of knowledge workers for whom meeting content is critical information that disappears without systematic capture.

The <a href="#otterAi">remote and hybrid team use case</a> is Otter AI's strongest application — teams that conduct business over video calls where no one is taking comprehensive notes, where decisions get made without written records, and where team members in different time zones miss meetings they need to catch up on. Otter AI turns every meeting into a searchable text record that any team member can access after the fact. The <a href="#otterAi">journalist and researcher interview workflow</a> uses Otter AI to transcribe recorded interviews and conversations — eliminating manual transcription work that would take hours per interview, producing searchable transcripts that make quoting and reference efficient.

The <a href="#otterAi">student lecture capture use case</a> applies Otter AI to classroom and lecture recording — transcribing professor lectures for studying, capturing seminar discussions for reference, and producing searchable notes from spoken educational content without requiring continuous manual note-taking during class. <a href="#otterAi">Business meeting documentation</a> provides the written record that organizations need for accountability, onboarding new team members, and institutional knowledge preservation — converting the ephemeral spoken meeting into permanent accessible documentation.

<a href="#otterAi">OtterPilot automatic meeting bot</a> joins scheduled video meetings automatically — no manual activation required when a meeting begins, transcription starts when the call connects. <a href="#otterAi">Vocabulary and name learning</a> improves transcription accuracy for organization-specific terminology, product names, and proper nouns — the specialized vocabulary that general speech recognition handles poorly becomes more accurate as Otter learns the specific terms used in an organization's meetings.

<a href="#otterAi">Mobile app companion</a> provides Otter AI on iPhone for transcribing in-person meetings, interviews, and conversations — extending meeting capture beyond video calls to any situation where spoken content needs to be documented. <a href="#otterAi">Team and enterprise plans</a> provide shared workspace access, centralized transcript storage, administrative controls, and compliance features for organizational deployment beyond individual use.

## 💬 FAQ

<details>
<summary>How accurate is Otter AI's transcription?</summary>

Accuracy varies with audio quality, accent, and background noise. Otter AI's accuracy improves as it learns organization-specific vocabulary and speaker voice profiles. For clear audio in good conditions, accuracy is typically high.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can multiple team members access the same transcripts?</summary>

Yes. Shared workspace plans allow team members to access, annotate, and collaborate on transcripts.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Otter AI work offline?</summary>

Otter AI requires an internet connection for real-time transcription and AI processing.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I export transcripts from Otter AI?</summary>

Yes. Transcripts export in multiple formats including PDF and text for documentation and sharing.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What languages does Otter AI support?</summary>

Otter AI primarily supports English. Check current Otter documentation for other supported languages.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Otter AI work for in-person meetings?</summary>

Yes. The mobile app transcribes in-person meetings, and the desktop application can capture audio from the Mac's microphone for in-room meetings.

</details>
